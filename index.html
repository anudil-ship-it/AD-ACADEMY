About Us

AD BIM Academy is a leading professional training institute transforming the way architects, engineers, and construction professionals implement BIM. Our programs combine theory, practical exercises, and real project scenarios to provide hands-on experience from day one.

We ensure every student confidently contributes to collaborative projects in Architecture, Structure, and MEP disciplines.

Courses

Revit Architecture

Master architectural modeling, visualization, and documentation for fully coordinated projects.

Revit Structure

Model structural elements with precision, coordinate with architecture, and ensure error-free workflows.

Revit MEP

Develop mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems with integrated clash detection and coordination.

Navisworks

Perform project review, clash detection, and 4D simulations for optimized construction workflows.

BIM Fundamentals

Learn BIM principles, standards, and collaboration methods for beginners or professionals seeking structured knowledge.
