About Us

AD Academy of Innovation is a professional training institute dedicated to delivering practical, industry-relevant education in Building Information Modeling (BIM) for the Architecture, Engineering, and Construction (AEC) industry.

Our academy was founded with a clear purpose: to move beyond software-only training and focus on how BIM is actually implemented on real projects. We emphasize workflows, coordination processes, documentation standards, and collaborative project environments that reflect real-world practice.

At AD Academy of Innovation, learning is structured, progressive, and hands-on. Participants work through realistic project scenarios that mirror professional responsibilities across architectural, structural, and MEP disciplines. This approach ensures that learners not only understand BIM tools, but also develop the ability to apply BIM effectively within multidisciplinary teams.

Our programs are suitable for students, early-career professionals, and experienced practitioners who want a structured and practical pathway into professional BIM implementation.

BIM Professional Training Program

Comprehensive Integrated Program
Our training is delivered as one complete BIM program, covering all key disciplines and coordination processes required in real projects. The program is structured to provide both discipline-specific depth and multidisciplinary understanding.

Architectural BIM

  • Architectural modeling using industry-standard workflows
  • Project setup, levels, grids, and worksharing concepts
  • Development of coordinated architectural models
  • Preparation of drawings, schedules, and documentation
  • Understanding model accuracy and design coordination

Structural BIM

  • Modeling of structural systems including columns, beams, slabs, and foundations
  • Coordination between architectural and structural models
  • Structural documentation workflows
  • Maintaining model integrity during design changes

MEP BIM

  • Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing system modeling
  • System-based design approach used in real projects
  • Integration and coordination with architecture and structure
  • Understanding MEP complexity in multidisciplinary environments

BIM Coordination & Clash Detection

  • Multidisciplinary model coordination
  • Clash detection principles and workflows
  • Identifying, managing, and resolving conflicts
  • Understanding coordination responsibilities within project teams

Navisworks & Project Review

  • Model aggregation and review processes
  • Clash detection management
  • Construction sequence understanding (4D concepts)
  • Supporting coordination meetings and decision-making

BIM Concepts & Project Workflows

  • BIM principles and industry practices
  • Information flow across project stages
  • Roles and responsibilities in BIM-enabled projects
  • Understanding how BIM supports construction delivery
