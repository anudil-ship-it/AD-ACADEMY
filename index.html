Courses

Revit Architecture

Master architectural modeling, from walls, doors, windows, and floors to detailed project documentation. Create fully coordinated architectural models, visualization techniques, and project-ready deliverables.

Revit Structure

Model beams, columns, foundations, and reinforcement with precision. Understand structural coordination with architectural models and produce error-free documentation.

Revit MEP

Develop Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing systems. Learn system integration, clash detection, and coordinated multidisciplinary project modeling.

Navisworks

Perform project review, clash detection, and 4D simulations to optimize construction workflows and enhance project collaboration.

BIM Fundamentals

Understand BIM principles, standards, execution strategies, and collaboration methods. Ideal for beginners or professionals seeking structured knowledge of BIM workflows.
